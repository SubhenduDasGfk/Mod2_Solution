Fish

A fish is an aquatic creature that lives in the water for its whole life. Fish have fins that help them swim in the water. They are found in rivers, ponds, oceans, and many other water bodies. Small fish feed on sea plants while big fish eat these small fish for survival.

Sushi

The word "Sushi" comes from the Japanese word "Su" (酢) meaning vinegar, and "Meshi" (飯), meaning rice. Sushi is made with specially prepared rice, called sumeshi (酢飯). The rice is mixed with vinegar, salt, and sugar.
